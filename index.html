Program Framework

A Two-Track Strategy

JSE executes its strategy through two synergistic tracks with State-Owned Enterprises positioned at the center — as partners, first customers, and investors.

View Details

SOE Challenge & Open Innovation

Startups apply to address predefined SOE operational challenges or propose innovative ideas to pilot within SOE environments. The SOE acts as the primary customer and Venture Client — de-risking adoption while guaranteeing commercial demand from day one.

  • SOE as Venture Client
  • Paid pilot contracts
  • EIH & SOE Direct investment
  • 5 startups/SOE
View Details

National Innovation Program

Startups building frontier ventures for the open market — without a direct SOE challenge brief linkage — receive the full JSE venture building infrastructure. SOEs participate as resource and infrastructure providers, contributing data, facilities, and domain expertise.

  • Sector agnostic
  • SOE as resource provider
  • EIH Direct, VCs, Angels, ESX
  • Maturity-based placement
