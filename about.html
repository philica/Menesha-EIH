We connect high-potential startups with SOE's

JumpStart Ethiopia (JSE) is a transformative 2-year pilot program that links high-potential Ethiopian startups with State-Owned Enterprises through strategic partnerships.

Objectives

What the Program Is Working Toward

State-Owned Enterprises face slow, expensive innovation cycles, while startups struggle to find their first enterprise customers to validate their products. JSE solves both of these challenges simultaneously by bridging the gap between the two.

Read More
  • 01

    Accelerate SOE
    Innovation Adaptation

    Leverage startups as the primary source of innovation to address operational efficiency leaks and explore frontier industries

  • 02

    Strengthen Ethiopia's National Innovation Ecosystem

    Provide structured venture building support, market access, and financing pathways to accelerate the commercialization and scaling of Ethiopian startups.

  • 03

    Generate Strategic Investment Returns for EIH

    Build a forward-looking, diversified EIH portfolio by developing frontier ventures in AI, GreenTech, HealthTech, and strategic sectors.

  • 04

    10% of Program Graduates Achieve Market Penetration

    At least 10% of program ventures reach sustainable market penetration and operational self-sufficiency within 2 years.
